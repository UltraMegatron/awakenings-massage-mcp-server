üé® Canva Integration Issues

Q: Canva connection failed or shows "Not Connected"
Solution:
  1. Verify your Canva Developer App is properly configured
  2. Check that the redirect URI matches exactly: http://localhost:3000/canva-callback
  3. Ensure your Canva app has the correct permissions (design:content:read, design:content:write)
  4. Clear your browser cache and try reconnecting
  5. Try the authorization process again from the main page
Q: Unable to create designs in Canva
Solution:
  1. Verify your Canva account has design creation permissions
  2. Check if you've reached any Canva API rate limits
  3. Ensure your access token is still valid (tokens expire after 4 hours)
  4. Try reconnecting your Canva account
Q: Design export failing or taking too long
Solution:
  1. Check your internet connection stability
  2. Verify the design exists and is accessible in your Canva account
  3. Try exporting in a different format (PNG instead of PDF)
  4. Wait a few minutes and try again (server may be busy)

üì± Social Media Content Issues

Q: Posts not generating or showing error messages
Solution:
  1. Check that all business information is properly configured
  2. Verify your phone number and address are correctly entered
  3. Restart the MCP server and try again
  4. Check console for any error messages
Q: Content doesn't match my business or location
Solution:
  1. Update your .env file with correct business information
  2. Ensure BUSINESS_NAME="Awakenings Massage Company"
  3. Verify BUSINESS_PHONE contains your actual phone number
  4. Rebuild the project after making changes: npm run build
